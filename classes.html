My Favorite High School Classes

During my high school years, I had the opportunity to explore a variety of subjects. Here are some of my favorites:

Game Design

Game design was one of the most exciting classes for me. In this course, I learned about:

  • The principles of game mechanics
  • Storytelling and character development
  • Using software tools like Unity

Ceramics

Ceramics allowed me to explore my artistic side through hands-on projects. I enjoyed:

  • Carving into my creations
  • Learning about glazing techniques
  • Firing my creations in the kiln

Web Design

Web design opened up a whole new world for me. In this class, I learned:

  • HTML and CSS for building websites
  • The basics of responsive design
  • How UX Design is important

English

English classes helped me improve my writing and analytical skills. I particularly enjoyed:

  • Reading classic literature
  • Participating in discussions and debates
  • Writing essays on various topics

Math

Math was challenging yet rewarding. I found joy in:

  • Solving complex problems
  • Learning about algebra and geometry
  • Applying math in real-life scenarios
